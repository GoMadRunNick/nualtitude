MadRun: From Niche to Mainstream

MadRun, an innovative athletic apparel brand, partnered with NuAltitude to break into the competitive US market. Our challenge was to build brand awareness and drive sales without a massive marketing budget.

We implemented a multi-faceted strategy focusing on TikTok Shop integration, a targeted creator seeding program, and performance-driven ad campaigns. By leveraging our creator network, we generated authentic user-generated content that resonated with their target audience, leading to a 300% increase in sales within the first quarter.

  • ✓ 5M+ Video Views from creator network

F&B Manufacturer: Direct-to-Consumer Pivot

A legacy food and beverage manufacturer sought to diversify its revenue streams by launching a direct-to-consumer (DTC) channel. They had a great product but lacked the digital expertise to succeed online.

NuAltitude provided an end-to-end solution, from Shopify store development and TikTok Shop integration to a full-funnel digital marketing strategy. We optimized their supply chain for DTC fulfillment and built a robust financial model to track profitability, enabling them to launch and scale their online presence successfully.

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ $1M in DTC Revenue within the first 6 months
  • ✓ 25% Improvement in gross margin from DTC channel
  • ✓ Successful Launch on TikTok Shop and Amazon
  • ✓ Established a profitable and scalable new revenue stream
